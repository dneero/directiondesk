Constant Sitting Can Kill You

American’s sit for 12+ hours per day which comes with a whole slew of negative health effects. Sitting for prolonged periods has been objectively proven to be the 4th largest cause of mortality.

Risk of Cancer is Greatly Reduced

Studies have linked prolonged sitting to a greater risk of colon, breast, and endometrial cancers. Research has suggested that regular movement boosts natural antioxidants that kill cancer.

Moving Supports Bone Health

Bones, similar to muscles, require regular movement to maintain strength. Low-level activity (like occasionally standing) helps improve overall bone health.
